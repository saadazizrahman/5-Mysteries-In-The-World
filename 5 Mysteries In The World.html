What Is Mystery ???

A mystery is simply an unsolved question and the attempt to answer it. In literature, this mystery is often a crime, but anything unexplainable can inspire a great mystery novel. Mysteries are typically told chronologically from the time the mystery is discovered to the instant it’s solved, but not always. Whatever the timeline, all mystery novels create the same questions for the reader.
